Privacy Policy.

Short version: we don't track you. Long version below.

01

Overview

LOREI Web Studio ("we", "us") built and runs this website. We keep data collection to a minimum: no analytics, no advertising, no data resale. This page explains the little we do handle and why.

02

Cookies

This site does not use cookies — not for analytics, not for advertising, not for anything else. That's also why there's no cookie banner: there's nothing to consent to.

03

Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Like any host, Vercel's infrastructure processes standard server logs (IP address, browser type, request timestamps) to serve pages and keep things secure. We don't use this data for tracking. See Vercel's own privacy policy for how they handle it.

04

Fonts

Typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts. Loading a font may expose your IP address to Google as part of that request. We don't use it for tracking or advertising — only to render the type you see on this page.

05

Contact Data

If you email us, we use whatever you share — name, email, project details — only to reply and discuss your project. We don't add you to a mailing list or share it with third parties.

06

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date below. Questions are always welcome.
